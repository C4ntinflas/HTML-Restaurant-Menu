Origin Story

Growing up, Alex's family often indulged in international cuisine, and Thai food, with its tantalizing mix of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy flavors, had a special place in Alex's heart. As the years went by, Alex became an avid traveler, exploring various corners of the world. During one of these adventures, Alex embarked on a journey to Thailand. The enchanting land, with its vibrant street food culture and warm hospitality, left a lasting impression on Alex's soul.
